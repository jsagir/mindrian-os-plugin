Create templates/meeting-report.html -- a Minto pyramid structured meeting report.

The template extends _base.html and has 5 levels:

Level 2 - Logical Claim (div.minto-claim): "What the Meetings Collectively Tell Us" -- synthesized narrative. Patterns, direction, key convergence themes.

Level 3 - Key Meeting Insights (div.minto-backbone): For each meeting sorted by date, a card with: meeting name + date header, speaker list with role badges, key decisions and action items, border-left colored with rotating De Stijl accent.

Level 4 - Contradictions and Open Questions (div.minto-evidence): Contradiction entries from intelligence data. Unresolved action items and pending decisions. Each with source meeting attribution.

Level 5 - Full Analysis by Meeting (page-break-before each): Meeting header with date and speaker tags (role-colored). Section-colored filing indicators per DOCS-06: for each artifact filed from this meeting, a colored bar matching the target section color + section label + artifact title. Summary content from summary.md.

- .minto-executive: large text, Mondrian red accent bar left
- .meeting-card: dark background, colored left border
- .speaker-tag: inline pill with role-appropriate border color

- Page breaks between Level 5 sections
