(5) When methodology produces its artifact, ensure pipeline provenance is in frontmatter (pipeline, pipeline_stage, pipeline_input fields). (6) Ask: "Stage {N} complete. Continue to {next stage}? Or take a different path?"
  - If user wants to exit: Summarize what was completed so far. Remind them they can resume later -- Larry will detect existing pipeline artifacts by provenance frontmatter.
  - When complete: Summarize the full chain -- what was produced, where it was filed, and what the provenance chain shows. Suggest next steps.
- IMPORTANT behavioral rules: (1) Pipelines are SUGGESTED sequences, not mandatory -- user can exit at any point. (2) Never modify previous stage artifacts -- each stage creates its own.
